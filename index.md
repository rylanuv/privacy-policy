Privacy Policy for HydraTrack – Water Reminder
Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Introduction
Welcome to HydraTrack – Water Reminder ("we," "our," or "us"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application.

By using HydraTrack, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide
To provide core hydration tracking functionality, HydraTrack requires the following information:

Profile Information: age, gender, weight, activity level, and preferred unit system (metric or imperial)
Hydration Preferences: daily water intake goal, cup size, wake-up time, bedtime, and reminder settings
Account Information: if you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name from your Google account

Automatically Collected Information
Water Intake Logs: timestamps and amounts of water you log in the app
Device Information: limited technical information required for app functionality
Usage Analytics: aggregated and anonymized app usage data collected through Firebase Analytics to help improve app performance and user experience

How Your Information Is Used
The app automatically uses the collected information to:

Calculate personalized daily hydration goals on your device
Track and display your hydration history and progress
Send hydration reminders based on your preferences
Sync your data across devices if you sign in with Google
Process in-app purchases and manage premium subscriptions
Improve app functionality and stability
Display widgets showing hydration progress

All calculations and reminders are handled automatically by the app. No human review of your data occurs.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage
Your hydration data and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure Android storage mechanisms such as Room Database and DataStore.

Cloud Storage
If you sign in with your Google account, your data is securely synced to Google Firebase Firestore. This allows you to access your data across multiple devices and restore your data when switching devices.

Data Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Data stored on your device is protected using Android’s built-in security mechanisms. If you sign in with Google, data synced to the cloud is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure and protected using Google’s industry-standard security practices. While no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information.

Third-Party Services
HydraTrack uses the following third-party services:

Google Firebase – authentication, cloud data storage, analytics
Google Play Billing – processing premium subscription purchases
Google Play Services – core app functionality and authentication

These services operate under their own privacy policies:

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

In-App Purchases
Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play. We do not collect or store payment information. Subscription status is stored locally and securely synced to enable access across devices.

Data Retention
Local Data: remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data
Cloud Data: retained until you delete your account or request data deletion

Your Rights and Choices
You have the right to:

Access your data within the app
Modify your profile information and preferences
delete your entire account from the app settings
Use the app without signing in, in which case data remains stored locally only

Children’s Privacy
HydraTrack – Water Reminder is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date.
