# Privacy Policy for HydraTrack – Water Reminder
Last Updated: January 28, 2026

## Introduction
Welcome to HydraTrack – Water Reminder ("we," "our," or "us"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application.

By using HydraTrack, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
To provide core hydration tracking functionality, HydraTrack requires the following information:

- Profile information: age, gender, weight, activity level, and preferred unit system (metric or imperial)
- Hydration preferences: daily water intake goal, cup size, wake-up time, bedtime, and reminder settings
- Account information: if you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name from your Google account

### Automatically Collected Information
- Water intake logs: timestamps and amounts of water you log in the app
- Device information: limited technical information required for app functionality
- Usage analytics: aggregated and anonymized app usage data collected through Firebase Analytics

---

## How Your Information Is Used
The app automatically uses the collected information to:

- Calculate personalized daily hydration goals on your device
- Track and display hydration history and progress
- Send hydration reminders based on your preferences
- Sync data across devices if you sign in with Google
- Process in-app purchases and manage premium subscriptions
- Improve app functionality and stability
- Display widgets showing hydration progress

All calculations and reminders are handled automatically by the app. No human review of your data occurs.

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
Hydration data and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure Android storage mechanisms such as Room Database and DataStore.

### Cloud Storage
If you sign in with Google, your data is securely synced to Google Firebase Firestore. This allows access across multiple devices and data restoration when switching devices.

### Data Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Data stored on your device is protected using Android’s built-in security mechanisms. Data synced to the cloud is protected using Google’s industry-standard security practices.
